About Us

We aren't just any public school orchestra program! Las Flores Middle School has an award-winning orchestra program and was named top middle school orchestra at the National Orchestra Festival in 2008. We since have achieved many more great accomplishments, such as Superior ratings from SCSBOA and Disneyland. Under the direction of Mrs. Benefield, we are destined for another great year of success!
